Can I use my own appraisal?

  • We require all owner-occupied 1-4 family properties to be appraised by an appraiser of our choosing.

  • You will only be responsible for the fees associated with the aforementioned appraiser.

Will a credit report be required?

  • Before the application process is completed we will be required to review your credit report. Your credit report needs to be obtained by and sent to us from your broker.

What information will be required?

  • A 1003 loan application form
  • A credit report (to be obtained by your brother)
